🎤 My Music Journey

My love for music began when I was just 14 years old. I used to listen to songs deeply, and my hands would naturally try to reproduce the sounds — even without knowing the instrument. I kept practicing, and slowly I started getting it right.

The first instrument I learned was the piano, which taught me musical structure and melody. Playing piano gave me the foundation of rhythm, harmony, and hand control — and it's still one of my favorite instruments to express emotions.

Later, I fell in love with the flute. Its soothing and peaceful sound connected with me on a deeper level. I spent hours perfecting each note, and now I can confidently play multiple Bollywood melodies and emotional tunes on it.

I also enjoy creating rhythms using anything around me — table tops, boxes, or even random objects. Music flows through everything, and I love turning sound into expression.
